Help

Press the Plus, Astrix, Hash button to show the other buttons (not for use on total entry screen). You can also drag from this button to another button.
Drag from the Plus, Astrix, Hash button to the Plus button to add two numbers together (e.g. 4 + 5 would produce 11).
Drag from the Plus, Astrix, Hash button to the Astrix button to multiply two numbers together (e.g. 4 * 5 would produce 20).
Drag from the Plus, Astrix, Hash button to the Hash button to clear the input box. (e.g. 11 # 4 would produce 4).
Drag from the Plus, Astrix, Hash button to the Pause button to enter a new total.
